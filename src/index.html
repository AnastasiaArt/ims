Our services

Nuclear Medicine Solutions

We provide access to the latest nuclear medicine technologies, these include synthesis and dispensing hot cells, radiopharmaceutical injectors and monitoring systems. We supply everything you need to support nuclear medicine systems, such as ancillary equipment, shielded doors, waste disposal plants and generators.

Barrier Isolation Solutions

Improve safety of applying nuclear technologies in medicine. We support you with high-quality stainless steel barrier isolator systems that preserve balance between the necessary environmental requirements for research and hygiene standards.

PET Tracer Production and Development Solutions

Implement simple and complex radiochemistry with AllinOne and Easy One Trasis synthesizers. One of their features is open software for free layout and sequence editing, flexible technical configuration and the collaboration platform. Your synthesizer can evolve with your research. These synthesizers allow to produce over 30 different radiotracers. Try comprehensive sets of cassettes and reagents that come in ready-to-use packs and contain all reagents in the right formulation and concentration for each molecule.

COVID-19 Solutions

We focus on helping our healthcare system clients worldwide to make their environment disinfected and safe from bacteria and viruses. SD Biosensor Standard Q diagnosis tests ensure quick and effective COVID-19 detection, while TemaSinergie UVD Robots reduce the diffusion of harmful microorganisms.

Radiotherapy and Uro-Oncology Solutions

Advance your radiotherapy technology use by utilizing dosimetry, quality assurance, hyperthermia, breast stereotactic and focal therapy systems. We propose solutions to perform highly effective radiotherapy treatment and supply our clients with patient transfer, positioning and localization units that combine ergonomic characteristics and beauty.

Marketing Solutions

Making the most of your healthcare supply business implies developing an effective marketing strategy. Boost your sales and brand awareness by applying marketing strategies that you can advance together with us.
